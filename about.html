Regulatory Compliance

Thunderforex acknowledges the importance of compliance with all relevant laws, rules, regulations, policies and standards. Thunderforex is committed to having strict management discipline and first-class controls in our compliance environment. As a regulated entity Thunderforex is required to meet strict financial standards, including capital adequacy and audit requirements.

Thunderforex holds client money in client segregated trust accounts. All client money is managed in accordance with the Client Money Handling rules and held separately to Thunderforex company funds. Client money is not used for any operational expenses or purposes.

Audit

Thunderforex has in place an independent, external auditor to supplement our operational processes and ensure regulatory compliance.

Confidentiality

Thunderforex maintains appropriate controls and monitors the transfer of confidential and sensitive client information within and outside the Company on a need-to-know basis. Thunderforex does not deliberately disclose client information unless this is upon request of the client, or as required to do so by law.
